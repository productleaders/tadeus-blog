## The seat-activity trap

Most teams measure adoption by counting active seats. That tells you who opened
the tool — not who changed how they work. The gap between the two is where rollouts
quietly fail.

## What to measure instead

- Where the tool **replaced** an old workflow
- Where people **quietly abandoned** it
- What **blocked** the ones who tried
